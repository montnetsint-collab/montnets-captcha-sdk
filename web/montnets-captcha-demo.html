How to integrate in 3 steps:
1. Include montnets-captcha-sdk.js in your page.
2. Call CaptchaSDK.init() once on page load, providing a tokenProvider and an onSuccess callback.
3. Call CaptchaSDK.show() from your submit button.

Customise UI text: pass a messageResolver function or a messages object to init(). Use CaptchaSDK.defaultMessages to inspect all available keys and their default values.
